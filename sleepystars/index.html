Sleepy Stars is a soothing musical app which opens with a night sky filled with stars that twinkle in rhythm to beautiful lullaby music.

The music is arranged in two parts, with the brightest stars following the melody line, and the softer background stars following the accompaniment.

This provides an enchanting and peaceful musical experience, perfect for bedtime, but it also helps to develop a child’s musical ear.



Sleepy Stars has lullabies from around the world, featuring 6 songs with original arrangements:

  • Cradle Song, Op. 49 no. 4, Johannes Brahms
  • Twinkle, Twinkle, Little Star (Ah! vous dirai-je, maman), traditional French
  • Berceuse, Maurice Ravel
  • Northeastern cradle song, traditional Chinese
  • Hush Little Baby, traditional American
  • Cielito Lindo, traditional Mexican

It is my goal to start a company which develops imaginative, musical, educational apps for kids. I am dedicated to making apps that aren’t just used for entertainment, but that provide thoughtful and creative ways to stimulate a child’s mind.

It is important to me that apps for children always be free of ads and free of in-app purchases. In other words, a safe place for your kids.
