Game design and musical arrangements by T. Layne Ringstaff.

Sleepy Stars is a soothing musical app which opens with a night sky filled with stars that twinkle in rhythm to beautiful lullaby music.

The music features lovely original arrangements, with the brightest stars following the melody line, and the softer background stars twinkling in time with the accompaniment. Shooting stars and calming color changes also help to soothe a child’s weary mind.

All this combines to provide an enchanting and peaceful musical experience, perfect for bedtime. It also helps to develop a child’s musical ear by providing visual cues to help identify musical parts.



Sleepy Stars has lullabies from around the world, featuring 5 songs with beautiful original arrangements:

  • Cradle Song, Op. 49 no. 4, Johannes Brahms
  • Cielito Lindo, traditional Mexican
  • Hush Little Baby, traditional American
  • Northeastern cradle song, traditional Chinese
  • Berceuse, Maurice Ravel
