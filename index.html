My name is Layne, and I'm a Software Engineer born and living in Chattanooga, Tennessee.

I was 10 when I started writing my first lines of code in BASIC on the Commodore 64, creating simple text based adventure games. That sparked a love for coding which never went away.

I also love writing and playing music, and have dreams of building musical apps and games for kids and/or writing the soundtrack to a really cool game. I love creating simple, useful things, and I enjoy the process of problem solving and different aspects of creativity it takes to get there.
