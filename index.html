My name is Layne, and I'm a Software Engineer born and living in Chattanooga, Tennessee.

I was 10 when I started writing my first lines of code in BASIC on the Commodore 64, creating simple text based adventure games. That sparked a love for coding which never went away.

I also love writing and playing music, and have dreams of building musical apps and games for kids or writing the soundtrack to an awesome game. I love creating simple, useful things, and I enjoy the process of problem solving and different aspects of creativity it takes to get there.

I plan on adding to this site the details of the various projects on which I have worked, but for now you can see my new game for tired kids called Sleepy Stars , or, if you prefer words, download my resume.
